PORTFOLIO

Bookmarked

Bookmarked serves as a single destination to store all your interesting data. Posts can be categorized into Entertainment, Research and miscellaneous, and can be directly opened into respective apps or browsers upon click. You can sync all your data using an account, and retrieve it on other devices.

The Stores App

Our app is an attempt at crowd-control in small and medium sized stores and making the process of buying essentials smoother and safer. The store owners can update item availability and peak times on our app. Additionally, we also aim at making this process of procuring essentials a time-efficient process by letting you send the shopkeepers the list of items you need along with a message. The shopkeeper can gather and pack the items you need and store it ready to move as you take your time to reach the store, collect it and pay for it.

Enigma 7.0

Enigma 7.0 was the seventh installment to the annual cryptic hunt conducted by IEEE-VIT. This app was basically a game where users competed to solve a set of 25 questions. Users could use hints, powerups using xp points which were generated every hour, along with a leaderboard to keep track of everything. This app is highly acclaimed for its UI.

Treeder

Users can track their carbon footprint of their food by capturing it. Users will be given points called Treedos based on their acts promoting environmental sustainability. People can plant trees by spending Treedos. Nature enthusiasts can sign up to plant trees all over the world and track the trees planted. The result is a community consisting of nature lovers.

Vocabulator

Add words in your inventory. Learn a new word everyday. Look up words in a single swipe. Group your words into categories.

FFDS

A dating system for colleges that was originally made for VIT, which matches you with students based on common empty slots in your timetables. Users can right swipe to start interacting with a particular person or left swipe if not interested. Students can make their profiles and share their social handles on it along with a bio and expectation tags.
